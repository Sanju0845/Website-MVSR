COLLEGE PROFILE

Maturi Venkata Subba Rao (MVSR) Engineering College was established in 1981 with affiliation to Osmania University. Currently, the college has B.E programs in Civil, CSE, ECE, EEE, IT, Mechanical, and Automobile Engineering, PG programs in CSE, ECE, and Mechanical besides MBA.

This college has been recognized by Osmania University as a Centre for pursuing research leading to the award of Ph.D. Degree in the Disciplines of CSE, ECE, and Mechanical Engineering. About 1000 students take admission each year.

Our Vision

To impart technical education of the highest standards, producing technically competent, confident, and socially responsible engineers.

Our Mission

To impart adequate fundamental knowledge, technical and soft skills to students.
To make the learning process exciting, stimulating, and joyful.
To create a climate conducive to an excellent teaching-learning process.
To bring out creativity in students.
To contribute to the advancement of engineering & technology.
To make a positive contribution to meet societal needs.
